# Product Engineer – Mid-Level

## About the role

At Starley, we’re creating **Cotomo**—**one of Japan’s largest voice-based AI platforms**—where people in Japan and beyond can form lasting bonds with anime-style characters, **voiced by real Japanese seiyuu (voice actresses)**. In a world where technology can now remember, feel, and even say goodbye, Cotomo invites users to build genuine connections with digital personalities.

With the ability to **design and customize your own anime-inspired AI characters**, Cotomo brings emotional depth and warmth to every interaction. Rooted in Japan, our philosophy is simple yet powerful: 「話したいことも、話したくないことも」—AI companions who are there for you, no matter the moment.

If you’re a product engineer who loves building end-to-end features—from backend logic to the final user touchpoint—and enjoys shaping products that blend emotion, storytelling, and technology—**join us in Tokyo** and help bring Cotomo to life.

## Responsibilities:

- Design and implement efficient, highly available infrastructure to support high traffic
- Build backend systems that integrate various AI models, including speech recognition, natural language processing, and speech synthesis
- Develop streaming systems to deliver high-quality, real-time voice communication
- Construct and optimize scalable frameworks for large-scale data processing
- Collaborate with product managers and designers to participate in product improvement and feature planning
