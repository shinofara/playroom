### **Product Manager, Communication AI**

## **Job Description**

At Starley, we’re creating **Cotomo**—**one of Japan’s largest voice-based AI platforms**—where people in Japan and beyond can form lasting bonds with anime-style characters, **voiced by real Japanese seiyuu (voice actresses)**. In a world where technology can now remember, feel, and even say goodbye, Cotomo invites users to build genuine connections with digital personalities.

With the ability to **design and customize your own anime-inspired AI characters**, Cotomo brings emotional depth and warmth to every interaction. Rooted in Japan, our philosophy is simple yet powerful: 「話したいことも、話したくないことも」—AI companions who are there for you, no matter the moment.

In this role, you’ll lead the strategy and development of Cotomo as well as future AI products. You’ll work closely with engineering, design, and marketing teams to shape features, define user experiences, and expand our global reach. If you’re excited to build emotionally resonant products and help shape the future of AI communication—**join us in Tokyo** and help guide the next chapter of Cotomo.

### **Key Responsibilities**

- Develop and execute strategies and tactics to create products that users love
- Lead product development in close collaboration with engineering, design, and marketing teams
- Drive preparations and strategies for global market expansion

### **Required Skills and Experience**

- Over 3 years of product management experience in consumer-focused products
- Japanese speaker or N2 level or higher communication skills
- Strong interest and enthusiasm for the generative AI field
- Experience leading or collaborating with designers to create wireframes, prototype designs, and conducting UI/UX validation such as usability testing using tools like Figma
- Experience with project management and schedule management

### **Preferred Skills and Experience**

- Experience in startups or launching new businesses
- Bilingual communication skills in English and Japanese
- Experience in product development focused on machine learning
